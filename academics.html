Our Academic Programs

Primary School (Classes 1-5)

Our primary program focuses on building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and social skills through interactive learning methods.

Subjects Offered:

  • English
  • Hindi
  • Mathematics
  • Environmental Studies
  • Computer Basics
  • Art & Craft
  • Physical Education

Middle School (Classes 6-8)

The middle school curriculum emphasizes conceptual understanding and application of knowledge across disciplines.

Subjects Offered:

  • English
  • Hindi
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Science
  • Computer Science
  • Sanskrit
  • Art Education
  • Physical Education

Secondary School (Classes 9-10)

Our secondary program prepares students for the RBSE board examinations while developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Subjects Offered:

  • English (Communicative)
  • Hindi Course-A or Hindi Course-B
  • Mathematics Standard or Mathematics Basic
  • Science
  • Social Science
  • Sanskrit

Senior Secondary School (Classes 11-12)

We offer three streams in senior secondary with specialized subjects to prepare students for higher education.

Science Stream

  • English Core
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Mathematics/Biology
  • Hindi

Commerce Stream

  • English Core
  • Accountancy
  • Business Studies
  • Economics
  • Hindi

Arts/Humanities Stream

  • English Core
  • History
  • Political Science
  • Economics
  • Geography
