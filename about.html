About Our School

Our History

Established in 1991, Maharshi Dayanand Senior Secondary School has been a beacon of quality education in Salarpur for nearly four decades. Founded with the vision of providing holistic education to the children of the region, our school has grown from modest beginnings to become one of the most respected educational institutions in the area.

Named after the great social reformer Maharshi Dayanand Saraswati, our school embodies his principles of truth, knowledge, and social service. Over the years, we have maintained a tradition of academic excellence while adapting to the changing needs of education.

Our Vision

To create a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, moral values, and social responsibility, empowering students to become global citizens while retaining their cultural roots.

Our Mission

  • To provide quality education that develops critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • To nurture creativity and innovation in every student
  • To instill strong moral values and social responsibility
  • To promote physical fitness and mental well-being
  • To prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century
