Admissions 2025-26

Admission Process

  1. Inquiry

    Visit our school or contact the admission office to get information about availability and procedures.

  2. Registration

    Fill out the registration form and submit required documents along with the registration fee.

  3. Interaction

    For certain classes, an informal interaction with the child and parents may be conducted.

  4. Admission Confirmation

    On meeting the criteria, parents will be notified and need to complete the admission formalities.

Admission Criteria

Nursery to Class 1

Age-appropriate admission based on interaction

Class 2 to Class 9

Based on previous academic records and school leaving certificate

Class 11

Based on Class 10 board exam results and stream selection

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate (for Nursery to Class 1)
  • Aadhaar Card of child
  • Previous year's report card (if applicable)
  • Transfer Certificate (for Class 2 onwards)
  • Passport size photographs (4 copies)
  • Address proof
